Works

In my artistic practice I employ a number of styles and techniques. I work with different types of printing methods, like lithography, drypoint, textile prints and experimenting printing on different materials. I also enjoy painting with oil and guache. About

Born 1987 in Tokyo, studied architecture and japanese literature. Currently works at Ålgården and KKV in Gothenburg, Sweden. She has exhibited her art in urban environments throughout the city of Gothenburg. See more of Chihiros works on her instagram profile @noderachihiro
